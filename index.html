A Ü T O / M Ö T O R

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R is a partnership between a creative studio, composer, and master projectionist, with contributions from digital artists and technologists.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R is a production studio.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R creates immersive environments inspired by physical experiences and reconstructive storytelling in the digital age. We make game-like journeys that evoke an alternative sense of place and experience.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R investigates the idea of wandering between physical and virtual worlds. We act as a digital messenger, translating experiences and messages into a virtual body, that lives infinitely as digital matter.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R current project is disrupting the way people interact with music. No longer can record labels expect an audience who passively streams new releases. We are contributing innovation that will lead people to demand an entirely new approach to the way we experience music.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R creates immersive experiences for digital and physical spaces. Our creative strategists, digital artists, musicians, technologists and distribution channels deliver art product for immersive experiences.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R first project is an album, titled A Ü T O/M Ö T O R.

This experiential album of music is a platform A Ü T O/M Ö T O R created to distribute a collection of songs and accompanying 3D digital artwork in virtual spaces accessible across multiple levels of immersion.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R creates the music, the mood board, and accompanying artwork. We work with media partners and distribution platforms that impact audience engagement.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R releases songs as interactive 3D websites because the internet is a public art space accessible to everyone around the world. We are liberating artists' visions to be instantly accessible to anyone who wishes to interact with their work.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R creates 360 video and VR installations for mobile so we can connect artists' visions with tangible art objects we can hold in our hands and wear on our heads.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R develops virtual music environments for distribution on Youtube, Facebook, IOS and Android apps, and for WebVR.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R virtual environments become physical spaces.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R VR CAVE activates 360 Video and VR content played seamlessly in any scale installation or room. We are also activate interactive installations using motion capture. Full immersion means the audience isn't just listening to a stream, watching a music video, or panning around a 360 image – you are inside the experience, driving the experience.

A Ü T O/M Ö T O R is excited to cultivate shared virtual experiences across digital and physical spaces for more artists.

Active engagement in publicly accessible spaces encourages the evolution of all expressions of digital art, and a user experience powered by the audience.

Projects such as the A Ü T O/M Ö T O R experiential album, coded environments, digital art, and sound design assets are available from our project library so music and 3d environments can be remixed by anyone who shares in the experience.
